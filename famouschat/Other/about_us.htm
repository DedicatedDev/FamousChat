Welcome to chatterli

chatterli is video chat booking app for influencers. You can select an influencer like famous actor and actree, scientist, etc, and book him. If the influencer will accept your booking request, you can video call on booked date.

chatterliuse "credits". All transaction is proceed with credits

You have to pay 10% of booking fee to "credis" system to book a chat

And, you have to pay 10% of auction cost to "credits" system to process an auction.

For booking an influencer, you have to do following:

  • You have to verify your payment account. Go to payment on menu, please verify your payment account.
  • On trend page(second tab), please select an influencer who you want. In other way, you can click "Category" button and select a category which you want, so you can select an influencer who matched with selected category
  • After second step, you will see booking page. Please select booking date and time, duration and input question what you want to know. If you click "Book Confirmation", you can book the influencer on selected date and time

On third tab, you can see your booked list. You can select a book and start video call there.

On fourth tab, you can see your booked dates. And You can search a influencer with name and book him.

On fifth tab, you can message chat with influencer or standard users.
